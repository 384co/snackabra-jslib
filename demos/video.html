Welcome!

384 is a new communication and storage platform. Current solutions suffer from either being 15-year-old+ architectures, or from problematic monetization models (“you are the product”), or both. 384 is web-first, edge-based, and ledger-backed. The from-the-ground-up technology is based on several years of research and prototyping, leveraging recent advances in crypto and cloud. The result is a new technology stack - a new “LAMP” in the modern world, or “S3 for Web3” if you will - with higher performance, higher security, genuine privacy, and lower cost. From the start we included modern notions such as data sovereignty, zero knowledge proofs, and micro federation.
